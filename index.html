About

We provide traditional Nadaswaram and Thavil music for South Indian weddings, temple rituals, and special occasions. With years of training and experience, we bring authentic mangala isai to make your event truly auspicious.

Our repertoire includes classical, devotional, and wedding-specific pieces tailored to each part of your ceremony — from bride & groom entrance to muhurtham and beyond.

Services

Wedding Ceremonies

Nadaswaram & Thavil for muhurtham, vara puja, entry, and reception segments, coordinated with your priest and event schedule.

Temple Functions

Music for kumbhabhishekam, utsavams, processions, and special alankarams in temples and home shrines.

Housewarming & Other Events

Auspicious music for gruha pravesham, anniversaries, naming ceremonies, and community gatherings.
